All illegal migrants will be evicted before next LS polls, says Amit Shah

Union home minister Amit Shah Wednesday said every illegal migrant in the country will be sent home before the next parliamentary elections, as he invoked the NRC, Article 370 and Rafale purchase during an assembly poll rally. He was addressing the first of his 3 public meetings of the day in Haryana, where elections to the state assembly will be held on Oct 21.

I am still young, will rest after packing off BJP-Sena: Sharad Pawar

NCP chief Sharad Pawar, who will turn 79 in December, said on Wednesday that he is still young, and will not rest until he defeats the ruling BJP-Shiv Sena in Maharashtra. The veteran leader was addressing a rally in Akola's Balapur ahead of October 21 state elections. Pawar, 78, has virtually become the face of the NCP's campaign.
